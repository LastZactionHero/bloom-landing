Gala Apple

A wonderful New Zealand, red, dessert apple with a distinctive yellow blush. Showy clusters of lightly scented blush pink flowers in spring which produces edible fruit in autumn. This early harvest variety requires a different selection of the same species growing nearby in order to set fruit. Good pollenizer for other varieties. Recommended chill: Less than 500 hours.
