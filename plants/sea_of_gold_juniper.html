Sea Of Gold Juniper

Outstanding lacy, golden yellow foliage on this compact evergreen makes a bold statement in the landscape. Superb color retention with a gold hue that deepens in winter better than other gold-tipped varieties. An excellent choice for mass plantings, along foundations, fence lines, driveways and island plantings. Colorful and Carefree. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Deer Resistant. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color. Compact Form. Tolerates Urban Pollution. Ornamental Berries.
