Tricolor Moses-In-The-Cradle

Interesting broad, sword-like leaves give this tender perennial a succulent look. Foliage forms a rosette of white, pale pink and green leaves with purple undersides. Typically grown as an annual in pots or hanging baskets, or as a textural accent in the garden. Drought Tolerant. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color.
