      <p>One of the earliest hybrid peonies to bloom! Crimson, single flowers sit atop sturdy stems and deeply dissected leaves. The rich green, fern-like foliage is inherited from its parent, Paeonia tenuifolia, and remains attractive through summer and fall. An American Peony Society gold medal winner. Herbaceous perennial. Showy Flowers for Cutting. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Gift Plant.</p>
