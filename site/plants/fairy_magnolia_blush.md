This upright, bushy shrub is noted for its masses of russet colored buds opening to lightly fragrant blush-lilac pink flowers in late winter to mid-spring. Its compact size makes it an excellent specimen or hedging plant. Plant near a doorway or windows to enjoy its fragrant blooms. Fragrant. Compact Form.</p>
