      <p>Create a stunning vertical effect with feathery stalks that emerge reddish-brown in spring and turn golden in fall. Blooms two to three weeks earlier than common feather reed grass. Thrives in wet soils, yet tolerates drier conditions. Stalks make great cut flowers. Sterile seeds do not produce unwanted seedlings. Showy Plumes. Fast Growing. Easy Care. Deer Resistant. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color. Fall Color.</p>
