      <p>This upright, compact lilac blooms later than others, extending the season with deep purple buds that reveal clusters of highly fragrant, lavender blue flowers. Foliage is burgundy-tinged in fall. Hardy, yet performs in southern regions, with excellent powdery mildew resistance. Great for border accent or mass planting. Deciduous. Compact Habit. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Improved Disease Resistance. Tolerates Road Salt. Compact Form. Fall Color. Bird Friendly. Improved Pest and Disease Resistance.</p>
