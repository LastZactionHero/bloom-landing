More cold tolerant than most varieties; typically blooms later in spring in colder regions. Upright, vigorous form grows quickly when young, filling in nicely as an evergreen hedge, screen or foundation shrub. Cold Hardy Camellia. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Pet Friendly.</p>
