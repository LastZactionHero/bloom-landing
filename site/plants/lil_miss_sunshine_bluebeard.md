      <p>Lil' Miss Sunshine Bluebeard is a new cross between Petit Bleu and Sunshine Blue that delivers the best attributes of both. This lovely plant has shiny yellow foliage that glows all season long and an abundance of blue flowers in late summer. The habit is neat and compact making it a great container plant and garden specimen. Attractive Flowers. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Deer Resistant. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color. Compact Form.</p>
