      <p>Especially suited to warm winter gardens! Anna produces large, crisp apples with light greenish-yellow skin and a slight red blush. Sweet, slightly tart flesh is perfect for fresh eating, cooking and baking. Produces at an early age. Self-fertile, however a pollenizer such as Dorsett Golden will increase production. Recommended chill: 200-300 hours. Edible Fruit. Fall Color. Bird Friendly. Edible.</p>
