      <p>Pendulous clusters of golden-yellow flowers and rich green foliage, gray-green undersides, grace this versatile, upright tree. Use to frame a home or entrance as well as line along a boundary as a screen or along a driveway. Note: All parts of this tree are poisonous (if eaten in large quantities). However, be aware that the fruit resembles pea pods and may be attractive to small children. Deciduous. Showy Flowers. Attracts Butterflies. Attracts Hummingbirds.</p>
