      <p>Snow white, extra large blooms with a semi-double, peony form. Showy flowers contrast well with the glossy, dark green foliage. Use as a specimen in a woodland garden, as a privacy screen or in a container. Flowers are lovely when cut and floated in a bowl of water. A prized plant of the milder regions. Mid to late season bloomer. Evergreen. Winter Flowering. Easy Care. Deer Resistant. Pet Friendly.</p>
