      <p>Large, firm blue fruit in early to mid-season. Vigorous growth and high-yield; bears fruit earlier than 'Bluecrop'. Burgundy foliage in fall, deep red stems in winter. Flowers provide nectar for pollinators and branches provide shelter and nesting sites for birds. Works well as a border or hedge plant. Semi-evergreen. Early Summer Berries. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. North American Native Selection. Compact Form. Fall Color. Bird Friendly. Edible. Showy Fruit.</p>
