      <p>The Let's Dance Hydrangea series represents the next generation of reblooming hydrangeas, selected for exceptionally vibrant flower color and sturdy stems. Moonlight offers large, vividly colored mophead flowers in summer, with the added bonus of great fall foliage color. Blooms every year on both old and new wood. Soil pH affects blossom color; blue in highly acidic soils and lilac to pink in slightly acidic to alkaline soils. Great massed, in containers, or as a cut flower. Repeat Blooming. Gift Plant.</p>
