      <p>A beautiful clumping, hybrid agave. Rosettes of glaucous blue foliage resemble a blue gas flame, with gracefully incurved leaf tips. The edges of the fleshy leaves often appear to glow when back-lit by late afternoon sunlight. This handsome, tough and drought tolerant plant lends a Mediterranean feel to sunny, dry gardens and is well-suited to containers. Waterwise. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Deer Resistant. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
