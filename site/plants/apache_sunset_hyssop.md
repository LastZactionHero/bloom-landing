      <p>This colorful Agastache fills a unique niche in the landscape compared with taller varieties. Knee high plants carry loads of flower spikes with deep orange flowers and rose purple buds all summer long. Blooms provide a striking contrast to the cool silvery, aromatic, finely textured foliage. Butterflies, hummingbirds, and songbirds adore this plant! Drought Tolerant. Attracts Butterflies. Deer Resistant. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds. Dramatic Foliage Color. Bird Friendly.</p>
