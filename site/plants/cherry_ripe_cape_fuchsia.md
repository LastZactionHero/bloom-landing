      <p>A South African relative of North America's Penstemon, Cape Fuchsia is one of the showiest and most adaptable of perennials. Wonderfully compact with a shrub-like form, this improved selection offers freely blooming, bright cherry-red tubular flowers, along with good cold hardiness. Perfect for smaller landscapes and flowering containers. Evergreen in mild winter climates. Showy Flowers. Fast Growing. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Deer Resistant. Attracts Hummingbirds. Compact Form. Bird Friendly.</p>
