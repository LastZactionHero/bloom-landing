      <p>The iciest pink blooms you'll ever see! Large flower heads of soft pink on shorter, sturdier stems than other varieties. Attracts beneficial, pollinating insects and butterflies. Combine with blue-toned grasses or shrubs for stunning effect. Perfect for late summer color in mixed beds, borders and dry rock gardens. Foliage dies back in winter, but its upright dried seedheads may be left to provide winter interest. Rock Garden Plant. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Waterwise. Bird Friendly.</p>
