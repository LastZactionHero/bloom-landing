Hummingbirds adore the coral-pink and yellow flowers that appear atop tall bracts in summer. Originally from Mexico, this low growing spreader thrives in dry, sandy soil, preferring cool sun to light shade. Showy Succulent Foliage. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Year-round Interest. Deer Resistant. Pet Friendly. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
