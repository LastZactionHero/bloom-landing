      <p>An excellent freestone peach with red-blushed yellow skin and firm, sweet yellow flesh. Ideal for fresh eating, canning and freezing. Attractive accent tree, with dark pink buds that open to fragrant pink flowers in early spring. Foliage turns bright gold in fall. Self-fertile, but will produce better if planted near another peach variety. Recommended chill: 800 - 900 hours. Edible Fruit. Fall Color. Bird Friendly. Edible.</p>
