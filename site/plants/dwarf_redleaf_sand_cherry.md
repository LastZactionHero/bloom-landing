While there is seldom a large crop, the small, sour fruit may be used in preserves and pies. Deciduous. Dramatic Foliage Color. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Dwarf Plant. Dramatic Foliage Color. Bird Friendly. Tolerates Urban Pollution. Ornamental Berries.</p>
