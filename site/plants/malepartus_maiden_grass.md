      <p>Unlike most that bloom in fall, 'Malepartus' sends up its flowers in midsummer, allowing cool-climate gardens to be graced with the full beauty of this ornamental grass: Flower plumes are held high above broad foliage with a superb erect habit. Blooms open pink, turn coppery red in fall and turn silvery white in winter. Bronze-red fall foliage adds striking contrast. Showy Midsummer Plumes. Easy Care. Deer Resistant. Waterwise. Fall Color.</p>
