      <p>A versatile, hardy and durable small shrub that produces an abundance of frosty white, buttercup-like flowers throughout summer. Finely textured green foliage on a mounding form. Thrives in garden settings but is widely adaptable to poor, rocky or sandy soils, and clay. Works well as a colorful accent in front of shrub borders. Deciduous. Durable Flowering Shrub. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Deer Resistant. North American Native Selection. Pet Friendly. Tolerates Road Salt. Waterwise.</p>
