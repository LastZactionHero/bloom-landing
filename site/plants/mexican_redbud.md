      <p>Great small space tree especially adapted for the conditions of the desert southwest. Brilliant rose-violet blooms appear in spring just before the foliage emerges. Glossy, blue-green leaves have pronounced wavy edges. Exceptionally showy autumn foliage is glistened with gold, resembling that of a quaking Aspen in late fall. Deciduous. Drought Tolerant. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Waterwise. Fall Color. Bird Friendly.</p>
