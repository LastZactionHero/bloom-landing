      <p>Brilliant golden yellow 3-inch, cup-shaped flowers bloom on this tidy mounding shrub. Attractive dark green foliage adds contrast to the blooms. Well suited as a low, untrimmed hedge. Stunning color when planted in mass. Grows well in dry, rocky soils. Summer Flowering. Deer Resistant. Tolerates Road Salt.</p>
