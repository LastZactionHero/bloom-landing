      <p>One of the most robust and reliable, this hardy native male fern offers superb texture with glossy, leathery, toothed foliage. Vigorous clumps are formed of many long fronds; up to four feet long in cool, shady sites. Keeps up appearances even in warm, arid climates, though plants are more compact. North American Native. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Year-round Interest. North American Native Selection. Pet Friendly. Waterwise.</p>
