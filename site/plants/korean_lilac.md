      <p>Upright, densely-branched, deciduous shrub with dark green velvety foliage in spring, and with a burgundy tint in fall. Soft pink to lavender flower clusters tip the branches for a lovely spring accent. It flowers about a week after the common lilac, extending the bloom season. Excellent for borders, screens and hedges. Deciduous. Compact Habit. Attracts Butterflies. Deer Resistant. Attracts Hummingbirds. Compact Form. Fall Color.</p>
