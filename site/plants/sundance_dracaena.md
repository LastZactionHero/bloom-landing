      <p>This New Zealand native has attractive green leaves with pink midribs and bases. The upper leaves are erect or arching; the lower leaves droop. Large panicles of small fragrant white flowers appear on mature plants in late spring and summer, followed by small white blue-tinged fruits. Forms a small palm-like tree, yet is often grown as a shrub or container plant. Offers a tropical look in dry gardens. Dramatic Foliage Color. Gift Plant. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color. Ornamental Berries.</p>
