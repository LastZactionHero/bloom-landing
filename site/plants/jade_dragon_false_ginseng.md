      <p>Collected by Dan Hinkley in the Yulong (Jade Dragon) mountains in Yunnan, China, this elegant and hardy selection offers a nearly unequaled texture to the garden. Makes a wonderful evergreen tree or large shrub for dappled shade. Its dense, rounded framework is clad with fine textured, glossy green foliage. Clusters of white flowers in mid to late summer yield black berries, providing winter food for birds. Summer Flowering. Year-round Interest.</p>
