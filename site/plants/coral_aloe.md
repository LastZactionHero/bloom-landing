      <p>This solitary succulent has flat broad pale gray-green leaves that vary in color depending on amount of sunlight. In full sun the foliage is pinkish and in shade more bluish-green. Very showy when in flower but also during the rest of the year due to its attractive foliage. A great selection for rock gardens or containers. Waterwise. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Waterwise. Bird Friendly. Tolerates Urban Pollution.</p>
