Gray colored bark exfoliates in thin strips to expose smooth, cream colored bark. This attractive small tree was introduced by Oklahoma State University. Deciduous. Summer Flowering. Attracts Butterflies. Improved Disease Resistance. Year-round Interest. Pet Friendly. Waterwise. Fall Color. Improved Pest and Disease Resistance.</p>
