      <p>One of the fastest growing and largest of the temperate clumping bamboos, this beauty from China is also possibly the most cold tolerant in its genus. Feathery green leaves and gracefully arching canes have new shoots that are pale blue in color. Clumps easily reach to 20 feet high, making an excellent hedge or screen. Clumping Bamboo. Deer Resistant. Clumping Bamboo.</p>
