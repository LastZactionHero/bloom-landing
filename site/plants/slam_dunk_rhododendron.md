A vigorous, cold-hardy hybrid developed at the University of Connecticut as part of their Raise the Roof series named to salute their championship basketball teams. A very effective accent or low, informal hedge. Evergreen. Showy Flowers. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Year-round Interest. Bird Friendly. Tolerates Urban Pollution.</p>
