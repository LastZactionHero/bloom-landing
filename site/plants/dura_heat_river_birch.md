      <p>A more heat and drought tolerant form of this species, with superior insect and disease resistance. Densely pyramidal form with glossy green leaves that fade to clear butter yellow in fall. Winter bark is pinkish-orange with prominent exfoliation. Large tree as focal point or for shading. Deciduous. Tolerates Wet Soils. Attracts Butterflies. Improved Disease Resistance. Year-round Interest. Deer Resistant. North American Native Selection. Tolerates Road Salt. Fall Color. Bird Friendly.</p>
