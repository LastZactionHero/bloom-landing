More cold tolerant than most varieties; typically blooms later in spring in colder regions. Upright, vigorous form grows quickly when young, filling in nicely as a hedge, screen or foundation shrub with handsome, dark, evergreen foliage. Cold Hardy Camellia. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Pet Friendly.</p>
