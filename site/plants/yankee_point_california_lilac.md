      <p>One of the best low growing ceanothus for use as a large-scale ground cover. Glossy, dark green leaves have a refined look giving the plant a handsome appearance. Deep blue flowers in 1 inch clusters. Thrives in rocky, salt-laden coastal settings, yet adapts well to garden settings and grows well inland if sited in dappled sun to part shade. Evergreen. Groundcover. Fast Growing. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. North American Native Selection. Waterwise. Bird Friendly.</p>
