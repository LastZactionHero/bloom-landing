      <p>Patty Bee' is a dwarf evergreen rhododendron with wavy-edged, funnel-shaped, pale yellow flowers. Small, dusty green foliage turns bronze in winter. Thrives in cooler regions but some protection from winter wind is helpful. Very effective in border plantings or as an accent. Spring Flowering. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Year-round Interest. North American Native Selection.</p>
