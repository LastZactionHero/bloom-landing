      <p>This amazing white-edged sport of Caryopteris Heavenly Blue forms a three foot tall and wide clump of woody stems, each clothed with finger-shaped white-edged green leaves. From summer through fall, the foliage is covered with rich, lavender-blue whorls of flowers that look stunning against the variegated foliage. Ideal in mixed perennial and shrub borders. Long bloom season. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
