      <p>One of the best cooking apples, Granny Smith has crisp, tart and juicy fruit with a good balance of acids and sugars. Medium-sized apples have pale to medium green skin with distinctive whitish dots. Requiring only 400 to 500 winter chill hours, and a longer season to ripen, this variety is favored in mild winter areas. Grafted onto M111 semi-dwarf root-stock. Deciduous. Edible Fruit. Fall Color. Edible.</p>
