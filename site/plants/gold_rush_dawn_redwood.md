      <p>Fast growing, pyramidal conifer has soft fern-like gold foliage that tends to not burn in full sun. In spring, the needles emerge nearly chartreuse yellow, hold their color well into summer, then turn orange brown in fall before dropping. Deeply fissured bark provides winter interest. Makes a fine hardy specimen tree. Year-Round Interest. Easy Care. Dramatic Foliage Color. Fall Color. Attractive Bark.</p>
