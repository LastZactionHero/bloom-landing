      <p>The first known gold-foliaged Cercis! Exceptional native redbud displays a riot of color in early spring as brilliant lavender-purple blooms appear on bare branches. Spring foliage emerges red, maturing to gold; shaded leaves will be green. Large, heart shaped leaves are burn-resistant even in full summer sun. Feature as a specimen or mass as an informal hedge. Dramatic Foliage Color. Attracts Butterflies. North American Native Selection. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
