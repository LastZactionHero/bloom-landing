      <p>A smaller clumping aloe, growing to just over a foot tall. Succulent leaves change from lime green to bright orange-red with white spotting. In mid to late winter, a slender flower spike rises above the foliage with dark flower buds that open to reveal greenish-yellow petals. Excellent for massing in water-wise garden beds. Great accent or small container specimen. Waterwise. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
