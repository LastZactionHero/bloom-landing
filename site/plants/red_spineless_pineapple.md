      <p>A beautiful, spineless relative of the pineapple with lustrous, coppery-red, arching, sword-shaped foliage. If grown in bright light, purple-white flower stalks will emerge throughout the growing season, producing clusters of attractive, inedible red fruits that resemble tiny pineapples. Ideal for containers and tropical mixed borders. Sensational Tropical Foliage. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Year-round Interest. Dramatic Foliage Color. Showy Fruit.</p>
