      <p>An old-fashioned favorite with a vibrant new look, this superb perennial displays clusters of heart-shaped deep red flowers from arching red stems over a long bloom season. Deeply lobed gray to purplish-green foliage forms neat, compact clumps. Pairs well with hostas and ferns that will fill in as the foliage retreats in summer. Herbaceous. Woodland Garden. Easy Care. North American Native Selection. Compact Form.</p>
