Dark bronze-red leaves and stems provide striking contrast to vibrant clusters of deep coral-red tubular flowers in late spring and summer. A must for the hummingbird garden! Performs well in containers. Use as an annual in cooler climates. Long Bloom Season. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Pet Friendly. Attracts Hummingbirds. Dramatic Foliage Color. Bird Friendly.</p>
