      <p>A dense and spreading compact shrub with handsome dark green, glossy foliage. This hardy mid-season bloomer heralds spring with deep red-violet buds. Big clusters of light purple flowers with lighter centers and vivid orange-yellow spots on the lobes nearly cover the plant with gorgeous dome-shaped trusses. Ideal for smaller gardens in containers, shrub borders and informal hedges. Compact Habit. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Dwarf Plant. Attracts Hummingbirds. Compact Form.</p>
