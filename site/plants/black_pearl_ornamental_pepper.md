      <p>Prized for its glossy black foliage and black-maturing-to-red fruit, Black Pearl grows vigorously in an upright bushy mound. Foliage emerges green but quickly turns shiny black. Small purple flowers appear in clusters in summer followed by small, round, pearl-like fruit. Although technically edible, the fruit is extremely hot! 2006 All-America Selection winner. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Dramatic Foliage Color. Edible.</p>
