      <p>This exceptional evergreen tree was selected from a Taiwanese collection by Dan Hinkley. Its tiered habit and glossy green foliage is without parallel. Chartreuse flowers that resemble tiny parasols appear in terminal clusters in early summer. A vigorous grower best suited to cooler maritime climates, it will quickly provide wonderful structure in the garden. Summer Flowering. Undefined.</p>
