Provides spectacular flowering and vertical interest when massed at the back of the perennial border. A good addition to meadows, native plant gardens, naturalized areas or stream margins. Vanilla scented flowers attract butterflies. Herbaceous. Patio Container Plant. North American Native Selection.</p>
