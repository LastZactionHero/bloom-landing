      <p>The lacy, aromatic leaves of cilantro are useful in salsa, sauces and roasting meat dishes, adding a nutty, citrus flavor. The mature seeds (coriander) create a culinary spice when ground, with a pungent citrus-rind flavor that is popular in many cuisines. Annual; gently self-sows if allowed. Edible. Pet Friendly.</p>
