      <p>Fragrant coral pink buds turn pure white in spring with green glossy crinkled foliage and dense branching habit on this compact rounded deciduous shrub. Aromatic with a pleasant clove-like spicy fragrance. Blooms mid-May persisting 10-14 days. Its slow growth rate and moderate size are appropriate for low maintenance residential scale landscapes. Disease resistant. May be semi-evergreen in mild climates. Fragrant Flowers. Improved Disease Resistance. Compact Form. Fall Color. Bird Friendly.</p>
