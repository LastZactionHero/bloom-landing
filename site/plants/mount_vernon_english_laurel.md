      <p>Unique spreading habit makes a lush, evergreen groundcover. Planted in mass, the shiny dark green foliage creates a clean, finished look under trees and large shrubs. This dwarf form of English laurel is rugged like its larger relatives, but does not flower or set seed. Takes shearing well. Works in poor urban soils, and dry shade. Compact Form. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Compact Form. Tolerates Urban Pollution.</p>
