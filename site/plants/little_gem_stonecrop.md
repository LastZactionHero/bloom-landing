Olive-green leaves turn coppery red in full sun. Cheerful star-shaped yellow flowers appear in late winter to early spring. Useful ground cover or container plant, indoors or out. Perennial. Waterwise. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
