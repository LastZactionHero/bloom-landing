      <p>This little-known agave relative makes a truly superb and unusual garden plant. Its glossy, evergreen rosettes form an attractive clump three feet wide. After it is established, it offers five foot tall red flower spikes adorned with lovely reddish bells with green tips in spring. Drought Tolerant. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Waterwise.</p>
