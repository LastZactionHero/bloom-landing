      <p>Chosen by the Chicago Botanic Garden for outstanding cold hardiness, this resilient boxwood holds its glossy green foliage well in winter. Resembling an English boxwood in appearance, it has a uniform oval habit making a fantastic low hedge, either sheared or informal. Excellent foundation plant or as background for perennials. Evergreen. Extremely Hardy. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Compact Form.</p>
