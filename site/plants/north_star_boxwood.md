Use North Star&#x99; as a low-growing hedge, or even to create the borders of a formal herb garden. It's a beautiful evergreen that will provide four seasons of enjoyment in the landscape. Compact Habit. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Deer Resistant. Compact Form.</p>
