      <p>Native to South Africa, this is one of the shortest, prettiest and most reliably cold-hardy species in the genus. Coral red and yellow flower spikes grow atop a thick clump of narrow, arching, blue-green foliage. Well suited to small city gardens and containers. Evergreen in mild winter regions. Showy Flowers. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Deer Resistant. Pet Friendly. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds.</p>
