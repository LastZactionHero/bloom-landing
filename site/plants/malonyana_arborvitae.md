      <p>Great choice for a quick, dense evergreen hedge or privacy screen. Shiny, mint-green foliage has a unique pineapple scent when crushed. Extremely narrow habit provides handsome vertical interest to any landscape. An ideal replacement for Italian cypress in cold climates. Discovered over a century ago, this European conifer is still rare in North American gardens. Evergreen Accent or Screen. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Bird Friendly.</p>
