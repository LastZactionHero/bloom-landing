The glossy, green foliage displays an excellent red fall color and is fragrant when brushed against or leaves are crushed. Works well in border plantings, backed by taller shrubs and perennials. Deciduous. Tolerates Poor Soils. Fast Growing. Easy Care. Waterwise. Fall Color.</p>
