Smaller rosettes often sprout around the base of the plant. Upon maturity, usually 8 to 15 years, the plant will produce a large yellow bloom in spring. Waterwise. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Deer Resistant. North American Native Selection. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
