Produces large black olives with low to moderate oil content, well-suited for canning. May be used as a pollinizer for Manzanillo. Thrives in hot dry locations. Drought Tolerant. Easy Care. Deer Resistant. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color. Edible.</p>
