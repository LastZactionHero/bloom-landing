      <p>Beautiful and unusual succulent shrub from Madagascar. Develops a bottle shaped trunk that is metallic grey and covered in needles. Trunk is topped with grey-green leaves with a mid-rib that turns bright pink in full sun. Fragrant white flowers with yellow throats appear on mature plants. Evergreen to semi-evergreen. Specimen. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Waterwise. Attractive Bark.</p>
