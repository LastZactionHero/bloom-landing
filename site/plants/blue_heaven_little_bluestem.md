      <p>Narrow, upright, grassy foliage makes a great vertical accent for a border, rock garden or container. Emerges blue, develops pink and burgundy hues in late summer, and turns a rich copper in fall. Slender stems rise up with purplish panicles. Tan seed heads persist into fall. Perfect for bird-friendly landscapes. Colorful Native Grass. Fast Growing. Easy Care. North American Native Selection. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color. Fall Color. Bird Friendly.</p>
