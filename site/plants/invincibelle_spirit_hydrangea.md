      <p>Invincibelle Spirit is the first-ever pink Annabelle hydrangea, producing loads of snowball shaped blooms from early summer to first frost. Dark pink buds open to hot pink flowers, fading to soft pink and finally to green. This hardy adaptable native selection is a reliable bloomer, even after the harshest of winters, and is also quite heat tolerant. Deciduous. Showy Flowers. Easy Care. Gift Plant. North American Native Selection.</p>
