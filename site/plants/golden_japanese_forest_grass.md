      <p>A graceful, colorful groundcover for shady areas., and great in containers. Foliage emerges in spring, with slender stems holding bright yellow leaves with thin green stripes having the effect of a tiny bamboo. Foliage becomes pink-tinged as the weather cools in autumn. Naturalizes well; clumps spread gently. 2009 Perennial Plant of the Year. Herbaceous perennial. Deer Resistant. Easy Care. Dramatic Foliage Color. Improved Pest and Disease Resistance. Tolerates Urban Pollution.</p>
