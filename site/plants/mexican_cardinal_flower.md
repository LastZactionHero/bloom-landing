      <p>Hummingbirds will flock to the showy red and yellow trumpet-shaped blooms on this North American native perennial. Fine textured foliage covers tall red stems that grow from underground rhizomes. Spreading habit makes it useful as a dense, large-scale ground cover or tall border. Great for warm season color, blooming nearly year- round in mild climates. Showy Flowers. Easy Care. Deer Resistant. North American Native Selection. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds.</p>
