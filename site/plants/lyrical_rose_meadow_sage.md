      <p>Exciting new perennial sage, with upright spikes of deep pink buds opening to rose colored flowers over a long season. Aromatic, rich green foliage creates a sturdy upright-mounded shape, nice in containers or massed in a border. Excellent heat tolerance, and reblooms readily to delight butterflies and hummingbirds. Showy Flower Spikes. Fast Growing. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds.</p>
