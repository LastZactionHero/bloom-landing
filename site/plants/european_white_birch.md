Toothy, diamond-shaped foliage emerges glossy green in the spring. Distinctive peeling bark makes an interesting landscape show, especially in winter. Deciduous. Attractive White Bark. Fast Growing. Year-round Interest. Deer Resistant. Fall Color. Attractive Bark. Bird Friendly.</p>
