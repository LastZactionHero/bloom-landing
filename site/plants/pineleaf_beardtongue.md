      <p>New, and perfect for sunny, low-water gardens, this compact perennial erupts in late spring with showy spikes of orange tubular flowers that have an inner glimmer of yellow, giving them a luminescent glow. The nectar-rich blooms attract hummingbirds and pollinators. Thrives in lean soils with little care, and pairs beautifully with lavender. Foliage remains evergreen, on neat mounded clumps. Waterwise Perennial. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. North American Native Selection. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds. Compact Form. Bird Friendly.</p>
