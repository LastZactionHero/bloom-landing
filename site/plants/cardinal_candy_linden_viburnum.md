      <p>Finally, even northern gardeners can enjoy the showy fruit on this hardy Viburnum! An attractive upright rounded shrub for hedge or foundation planting, displaying clusters of highly fragrant creamy white, lace-like flowers in late spring. Bright red berries ripen in fall and persist through winter, providing food for birds and wildlife. Handsome dark green leaves turn shades of bronze and burgundy-red in the fall. Deciduous. Extra Hardy. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Deer Resistant. Fall Color. Bird Friendly. Ornamental Berries.</p>
