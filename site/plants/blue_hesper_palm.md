Native to Baja California, this palm is slow growing and tolerant of rocky soils and desert heat. Maintains its unique blue coloring best in full sun. Evergreen. Waterwise. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. North American Native Selection. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
