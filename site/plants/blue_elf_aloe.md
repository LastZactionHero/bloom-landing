A favorite of hummingbirds, this minimal-care succulent thrives in poor soil and is perfect for rock gardens, water-wise borders or containers. Excellent mass planting. Foliage tips may have a reddish tinge in extreme temperatures. Evergreen. Waterwise. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds. Tolerates Urban Pollution.</p>
