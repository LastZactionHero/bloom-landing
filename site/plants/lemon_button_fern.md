      <p>Smallest of the Boston ferns. Cute and compact, tiny golden-green button-like leaflets on dark green, arching stems that reach just a foot high. Suitable as a houseplant or terrarium planting. Feature in atrium or shade beds in frost-free areas. A lemony scent is often expressed when the leaves are crushed. Evergreen. Shade Loving. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Pet Friendly. Dramatic Foliage Color. Compact Form.</p>
