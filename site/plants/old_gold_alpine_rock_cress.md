      <p>A unique variation on the familiar, this colorful rock cress has shiny deep green and gold-streaked leaves. Forming a dense, very low carpet of foliage, this is a choice plant for use as a ground cover, edging, or tucked between paving stones. Excellent rock garden or alpine trough accent. Drought tolerant, once established. Evergreen. Colorful Ground Cover. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. Deer Resistant. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
