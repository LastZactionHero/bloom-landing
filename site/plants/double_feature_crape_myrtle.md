      <p>This compact re-bloomer loves the heat and dazzles the garden with an abundance of ruby-red flowers. Wine-red new spring foliage is followed by summer blooms that produce no seed capsules, continuing to bloom on the same panicles right through to fall! This easy-care, water-wise shrub forms a dense, nearly perfect ball with little to no pruning. Deciduous. Repeat Blooming. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Dwarf Plant. Pet Friendly. Waterwise. Compact Form. Fall Color.</p>
