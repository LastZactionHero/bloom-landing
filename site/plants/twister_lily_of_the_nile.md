      <p>This new dwarf variety is an easy choice for edging, mass planting, or containers. Tall slender stalks emerge from clumps of strap-like foliage in mid-summer, each topped by a wide cluster of flowers. The creamy white buds reveal gorgeous bi-colored trumpet-shaped flowers of pure white and dark blue. Hardy to USDA zone 7b. Dramatic Flower Color. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Dwarf Plant. Attracts Hummingbirds.</p>
