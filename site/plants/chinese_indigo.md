      <p>Resembling a diminutive, ground-hugging wisteria, this exceptionally large flowered form was selected by explorer Dan Hinkley. A polite, low growing shrub, clad with bright green foliage, continuously presenting long clusters of pale, sweat-pea like flowers from mid to late summer. Prolific blooming habit and self-cleaning flowers provide a long season of beauty with little effort. Long Bloom Season. Undefined.</p>
