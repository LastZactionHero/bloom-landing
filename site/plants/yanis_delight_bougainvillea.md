      <p>Pink bracts tinged with white cover this attractive smaller mounding Bougainvillea. Lovely planted against a sunny wall or in a patio container, where it will provide fantastic color from spring through fall. Can be used as summer annual in cold climates in patio containers, hanging baskets; evergreen in frost free areas. Beautiful Summer Color. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds. Bird Friendly.</p>
