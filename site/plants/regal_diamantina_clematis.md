      <p>This award-winning, early-flowering clematis produces double flowers up to 6 inches wide. Blooms prolifically early in the season, repeating modestly in summer and fall. Excellent compact form for container or garden. Train on arbor, fence or trellis, or leave unsupported and allow to climb through shrubbery. Deciduous. Compact Flowering Vine. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Compact Form. Bird Friendly.</p>
