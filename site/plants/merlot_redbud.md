      <p>A perfect spring flowering tree for smaller landscapes, this phenomenal new hybrid raises the bar. Lustrous, dark purple foliage is similar to Forest Pansy, but with Texas Redbud in its genes, 'Merlot' offers excellent drought tolerance and thicker, glossier leaves that stand up to summer heat. Tight, dense, semi-upright habit. Deciduous. Drought Tolerant. Easy Care. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
