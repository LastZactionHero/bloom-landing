      <p>This rugged, southwestern native is ideal for low-water, easy-care landscapes. Glossy, aromatic foliage on a large, wide-branching form. Useful for slope stabilization and firescaping. Nice specimen shrub or small tree, or massed as an easy, informal hedge. Showy flower clusters produce fuzzy red berries. Hardy to 10&#xB0;F once established. Evergreen. Drought Tolerant. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. North American Native Selection. Waterwise. Bird Friendly. Ornamental Berries.</p>
