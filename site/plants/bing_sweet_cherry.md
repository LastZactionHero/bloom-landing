      <p>The most popular sweet cherry variety, know for producing exceptionally large, dark mahogany fruit that is firm, sweet and juicy. Excellent for eating fresh, canning or preserves. Grafted onto vigorous, disease resistant Mahaleb Cherry rootstock. Plant a pollenizer such as Stella or Rainier for best production. Recommended winter chill: 700 - 900 hours. Deciduous. Edible Fruit. Attracts Butterflies. Improved Disease Resistance. Fall Color. Bird Friendly. Edible.</p>
