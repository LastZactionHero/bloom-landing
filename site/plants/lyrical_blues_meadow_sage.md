      <p>Exciting new perennial sage, with upright spikes of burgundy buds opening to deep, violet-blue flowers over a long season. Sturdy mounding habit is more compact than 'May Night', with richer flower color. Excellent heat tolerance, and reblooms readily to delight butterflies and hummingbirds. Nice in containers, or massed in a border. Vivid Flower Spikes. Fast Growing. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds. Compact Form.</p>
