This variety produces little to no viable seed, making it a great alternative over others. Perfect size for edging as a small hedge or for containers. Deciduous. Colorful Dwarf Shrub. Deer Resistant. Dwarf Plant. Tolerates Road Salt. Dramatic Foliage Color. Fall Color. Tolerates Urban Pollution.</p>
