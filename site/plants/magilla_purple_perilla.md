      <p>Magilla Purple features showy coleus-like foliage, but with studier, woodier stems. The leaves are deep purple with a pink stripe down the center. A wonderful addition to large mixed containers or for adding contrast to landscape beds. May be grown in sun or part shade, however will put on best show in full sun. Very heat and cold tolerant showy foliage. Easy Care. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
