      <p>A "miracle" breakthrough in Heuchera breeding! This sister seedling of 'Tiramisu' produces pink flowers in midsummer, which is very unusual for a Heuchera villosa, as most have cream colored flowers. During cooler spring and fall, the foliage is a striking brick red with bright chartreuse to gold edges and silvered undersides. During warmer summer, red pigments lighten and the leaves turn green. Hybridized by Thierry Delabroye of France. Dramatic Foliage Color. Easy Care. Gift Plant. North American Native Selection. Attracts Hummingbirds. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
