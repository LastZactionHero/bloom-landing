This describes Santa Barbara, a large, superb tasting yellow peach with fine texture that melts in your mouth. Best for fresh eating. Self-fertile. Requires only 300 hours of winter chilling to produce a good crop, ripening in mid-summer. Deciduous. Low Chill Requirement. Edible. Low Chill Requirement.</p>
