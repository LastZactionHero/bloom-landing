      <p>This highly ornamental dwarf peach reaches just five feet tall in ten years. Double pink-red flowers arrive in very early spring, and dramatic, deep red leaves create a canopy of gracefully drooping foliage that holds its color well. Tiny fruits may appear, though not palatable, and often hidden by the dense foliage that this shrub is prized for. Excellent landscape or container accent. Deciduous. Spring flowering. Easy Care. Dramatic Foliage Color. Compact Form. Bird Friendly.</p>
