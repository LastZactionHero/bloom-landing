      <p>An exceptional compact form of the Honey Bush from South Africa, with sensational steely blue foliage with purple highlights, held along tidy stems to two and two and one-half feet tall. Erect burgundy red, nectar-rich bird- attracting flowers appear in late winter to early spring in mild regions. A wonderful container plant in colder regions. Prefers full sun and well-drained soil. Drought tolerant once established. Deer Resistant. Dwarf Plant. Dramatic Foliage Color. Compact Form. Bird Friendly.</p>
