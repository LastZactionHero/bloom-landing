One of the hardiest and most dependable, this classic Ironclad rhododendron is sun and shade tolerant, but thrives as an understory shrub in a dappled sun position. Evergreen. Spring Flowers. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Year-round Interest. Bird Friendly.</p>
