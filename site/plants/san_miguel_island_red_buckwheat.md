      <p>One of the showiest, this rugged perennial nearly thrives on neglect! A low mound of soft grey-green foliage displays hot pink flowers atop tall stems for a summer-long show. Tolerates drought, coastal conditions, alkaline and clay soils. An attractive low sub-shrub for hillsides and natural gardens, providing a valuable food source for butterflies, birds and other wildlife. Drought Tolerant. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Waterwise.</p>
