The large, petunia-like blooms are backed by dense clumps of narrow, dark green foliage. Quickly fills in as a ground cover. Drought tolerant when established, in cooler summer climates. Herbaceous perennial. Easy Care Plant. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Dwarf Plant. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds.</p>
