      <p>This architectural plant develops a short, thick trunk, while reaching a mature size of 6' tall by 5' wide. Each leaf ends in a wisp of fibers. Creamy white flowers appear in a showy plume that grows to 10 feet tall. Native to central Mexico; drought tolerant but requires well-drained soil and full sun. Patio Container Plant. Attracts Butterflies. Year-round Interest. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds.</p>
