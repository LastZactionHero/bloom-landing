      <p>This low, mounding shrub has thick green leaves and clean white flower clusters. A heavy spring bloomer, 'Popcorn' is a great selection for the garden, spreading to form a beautiful, dense groundcover that requires little maintenance. Good substitute for cotoneaster. Evergreen. Drought Tolerant. North American Native Selection. Waterwise.</p>
