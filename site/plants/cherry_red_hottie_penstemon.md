A drought tolerant perennial, native to the mountains of the desert southwest. A great plant for perennial borders, rock gardens and cut flower displays. Blooms will entice hummingbirds, butterflies and other pollenizers to visit. Waterwise. Attracts Butterflies. North American Native Selection. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds. Compact Form.</p>
