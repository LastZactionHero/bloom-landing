      <p>Summer Wine combines the fine texture and compact branching of Physocarpus Nana with the dark foliage of Physocarpus Diabolo. Season long purple foliage is covered with soft pink flowers in late summer. Adaptable and easy to grow, with excellent resistance to powdery mildew, this native is a useful landscape shrub and also makes an attractive cut flower. Dramatic Foliage Color. Improved Disease Resistance. North American Native Selection. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
