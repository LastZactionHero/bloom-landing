      <p>Lemon yellow, crepe paper blossoms shimmer in the warmth of the sun, and at night emit a lovely fragrance. Low, compact clumps of lacy, silver green foliage are tough and drought tolerant when established, making this a very useful perennial in areas with lean, sandy soils and a great selection for dry mountain states. Drought Tolerant. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Deer Resistant. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds. Compact Form.</p>
