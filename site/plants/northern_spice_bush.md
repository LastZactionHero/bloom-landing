Female plants may produce brilliant red fruit in early fall if planted near a male plant. Useful in shrub borders or woodland gardens, moist areas along streams or ponds, shaded native or naturalized plantings. Aromatic Foliage. Easy Care. North American Native Selection. Bird Friendly. Edible.</p>
