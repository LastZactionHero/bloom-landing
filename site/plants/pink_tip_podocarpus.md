      <p>This upright evergreen conifer has dark green needles with pink new growth. Its leathery leaves are close-set and spirally arranged. It is a particularly hardy, low growing and almost prostrate groundcover. Heat tolerant, and thrives in sun to shade. Wonderful as a filler for growing over walls and banks or in a large rock garden. Dwarf Habit. Easy Care. Deer Resistant. Dwarf Plant. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
