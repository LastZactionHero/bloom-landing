      <p>This trailing perennial is a prolific bloomer that will grace your garden with pink blooms that change to white from June through October. Variegated foliage adds to the color. As the leaves age to all green, cut back often to encourage new growth. Makes a great container plant or groundcover and also perfect in hanging baskets. Attracts Butterflies. Gift Plant. Deer Resistant. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
