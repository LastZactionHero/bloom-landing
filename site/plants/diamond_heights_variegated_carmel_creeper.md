      <p>Bright, low-growing evergreen shrub with dark green splashed chartreuse leaves -- exceptional variegation for a Ceanothus! Yellow tones dominate warm weather, while green is more dominant in cooler weather or shade. Clusters of pale blue spring flowers. Takes cool coastal sunlight; site in bright shade in hot, inland regions. Stunning in containers. Groundcover. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. North American Native Selection. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
