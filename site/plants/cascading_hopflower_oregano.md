Pale green and blush colored paper-lantern like bracts dangle from arching wiry stems, each embellished with a tiny rose-pink flower. Beautifully featured at the edge of raised beds and berms or in window boxes. Flowers are prized for dried arrangements. Herbaceous. Fragrant foliage. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds.</p>
