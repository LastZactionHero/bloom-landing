      <p>A stunning newer clematis variety especially suited for a container but wonderful in a landscape. Blooms on both old and new growth against green foliage with downy undersides. Showing off as many as 15-18 large flowers at a time, the gorgeous flower petals overlap with wavy margins and have a lovely light fragrance. Persistent seed heads add unique beauty. Deciduous. Compact Flowering Vine. Fast Growing. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Bird Friendly.</p>
