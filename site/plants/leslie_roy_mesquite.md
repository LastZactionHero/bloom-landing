      <p>Combining the best traits of two desert trees, this hybrid mesquite is remarkably thorn-free with a picturesque upright branching habit. Tolerant of extreme heat and cold, its blue-green, fern-like foliage casts welcome dappled shade beneath its canopy. Perfectly suited for arid Southwest, this is a stunning accent for desert landscapes. Semi-evergreen. Drought Tolerant Specimen. Fast Growing. Easy Care. North American Native Selection. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds. Attractive Bark.</p>
