In summer, older rosettes produce thick stalks topped with clusters of small pink blooms, then die after flowering while the younger rosettes continue to multiply. Adds rich texture and color to rock gardens. Ideal in containers. Rock Garden Plant. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color. Compact Form.</p>
