      <p>A fuss-free shrub with evergreen foliage on upright branches that softly arch as it matures. Produces bright yellow, sweetly fragrant flowers in late spring. A beautiful specimen when singly planted, however, when planted in mass, it produces a virtual avalanche of color. A great plant for sunny, dry hillside plantings. Carefree and Colorful. Fast Growing. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Tolerates Road Salt. Waterwise. Bird Friendly.</p>
