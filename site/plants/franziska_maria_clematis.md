Perfect for growing in containers in a smaller garden; equally outstanding in a larger landscape climbing a wall or mingled with taller variegated shrubs. Deciduous. Compact Flowering Vine. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Compact Form. Bird Friendly.</p>
