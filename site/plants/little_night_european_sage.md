      <p>A charming but tough little bloomer with deep purple-violet flower spikes covering the tight, mounding foliage in late spring, often repeating in late summer. Adds bold, vibrant color when massed in the fronts of beds and borders. Ideal for containers, rock gardens, and naturalized areas. A compact, herbaceous perennial. Drought tolerant when established. Waterwise. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Dwarf Plant. Waterwise. Compact Form.</p>
