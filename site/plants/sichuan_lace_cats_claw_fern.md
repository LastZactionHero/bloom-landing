      <p>Lacy, dark green fronds resemble that of a carrot top and sprout from trailing rhizomes which is why it's often referred to as the carrot fern. In the wild, it grows on rocky cliffs to 14,000'. Unlike other ferns, 'Sichuan Lace' thrives in a lightly shaded or dappled sunlight woodland garden, rather than deep shade. Deciduous. Woodland garden plant. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Pet Friendly.</p>
