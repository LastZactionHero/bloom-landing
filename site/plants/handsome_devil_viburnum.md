This compact, dense shrub makes an excellent hedge or specimen. Pure white June flowers are followed by red fruit which matures to black. For best fruiting, use V. odoratissimum as a pollinator. Dramatic Foliage Color. Easy Care. Deer Resistant. Dramatic Foliage Color. Compact Form. Fall Color. Bird Friendly. Ornamental Berries.</p>
