      <p>This fantastic compact version of Tuff Stuff Hydrangea will provide months of enjoyment in the garden! Lovely reddish-pink lacecap flowers create a mass of color in early summer, and continue to bloom right up until frost. The semi-double to double florets begin with creamy coloration in the center before maturing to intense pink. Flowers may shift to blue in acidic soils. Deciduous. Summer Flowering. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Dwarf Plant. Compact Form.</p>
