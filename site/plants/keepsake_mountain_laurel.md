      <p>Dark bluish green glossy foliage. Raspberry red buds open to abundant Burgundy Wine blooms with a thin white edge. Blooms about a week later than most other varieties. A partial shade woodland edge is an ideal location. Flowers are a nectar source for pollinators and evergreen foliage provides shelter for birds. Spring Flowering. Deer Resistant. North American Native Selection. Attracts Hummingbirds. Compact Form. Bird Friendly.</p>
