      <p>Coconut Punch produces an abundance of fragrant, intricately patterned, 11/2- to 2-inch double blooms. Flowers open deep red with white blotches in the center of each petal. As the blooms mature, the white blotches expand and the red coloration lightens, leaving a wine-colored ring around each petal. One of five cultivars in the Fruit Punch series selected for its heat and humidity tolerance and colorful double blossoms. Ideal perennial for containers, borders or mass plantings. Cottage Garden Plant. Attracts Butterflies. Gift Plant. Deer Resistant.</p>
