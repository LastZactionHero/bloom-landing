      <p>Tall spires of brilliant violet blue flowers rise above a tuft of large, deeply cut leaves. Slightly shorter stature reduces need for staking. Essential of the perennial border and cottage gardens. Performs in areas with short growing seasons. Bloom often continues through late summer into fall. Herbaceous perennial. Flowers for Cutting. Fast Growing. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Attracts Hummingbirds. Bird Friendly.</p>
