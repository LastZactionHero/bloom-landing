      <p>This award-winning perennial produces a multitude of pure white flowers on graceful, branching upright stems for five weeks or more, above a solid clump of dark green deeply cut leaves. Lovely massed in a woodland setting or spotted into wild rock gardens and an excellent cut flower. Naturalizes by spreading rhizomes in optimum conditions. Herbaceous perennial. Showy Summer Flowers. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Deer Resistant.</p>
