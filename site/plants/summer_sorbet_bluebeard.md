This strong selection has exhibited winter hardiness in USDA Zone 5; stems may die back and re-sprout from roots in spring. Fragrant foliage. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds. Compact Form. Bird Friendly.</p>
