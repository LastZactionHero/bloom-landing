      <p>This picturesque, cold hardy conifer makes an attractive centerpiece for the natural or woodland garden. Irregular, sweeping branches display tight bundles of short dark green needles and purplish-brown cones. Extremely slow growing; this shrub-like tree will maintain its size for years after planted in the garden. Ideal as a bonsai specimen. Native to the mountain regions of the southwestern United States. Drought tolerant. Native Plant. Easy Care. North American Native Selection. Waterwise.</p>
