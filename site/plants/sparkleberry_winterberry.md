      <p>Oval to rounded, deciduous shrub with rich, deep green foliage. Bright red berries appear from late summer to early fall. New foliage growth emerges showy red. Grows best in moist, rich, acidic, well-drained soil, yet tolerant of wet soils as well. Best flower and berry production in full sun. Requires a male pollinator for good fruiting. Attracts Birds. Easy Care. Deer Resistant. North American Native Selection. Tolerates Road Salt. Dramatic Foliage Color. Bird Friendly. Ornamental Berries.</p>
