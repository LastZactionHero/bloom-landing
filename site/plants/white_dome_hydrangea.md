      <p>This hardy, strong growing hydrangea rewards gardeners with clouds of big, lacy white blooms in summer. The dome-shaped flowers are 6 to 10 inches across and are held on sturdy non-drooping stems. Excellent massed in perennial or shrub borders, as a specimen, and in winter gardens. Dried flowers make distinctive arrangements. Showy Flowers. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Gift Plant. North American Native Selection.</p>
