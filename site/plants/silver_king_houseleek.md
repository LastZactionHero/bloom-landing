      <p>A garden jewel! Symmetrical rosettes of silvery mint green leaves have deep red-purple centers. Foliage color intensifies in cold weather, contrasting boldly with frost and snow. Offsets form to create a dense richly textured mat. In summer the older rosettes produce clusters of pink blooms on erect stems. Evergreen. Drought Tolerant. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Gift Plant. Deer Resistant. Waterwise. Dramatic Foliage Color.</p>
