      <p>A new waterwise perennial hybrid with the appearance and fast growth of rudbeckia and the hardiness and disease tolerance of echinacea! The yellow petals start with a red ringed center. By November the red is flecked throughout. Flowers are huge on compact plants - and can last two to three months. An herbaceous perennial. Long Bloom Season. Fast Growing. Attracts Butterflies. Easy Care. Improved Disease Resistance. North American Native Selection. Waterwise. Compact Form. Bird Friendly.</p>
