      <p>Exceptional selection of western redbud with rich, dark pink flowers that explode in profusion in late winter to early spring, followed by distinctive thick blue-green leaves. Yellow fall foliage is accented by maroon seedpods that may linger through winter. An outstanding form as compared to the species, selected at Rancho Santa Ana Botanic Garden. Ideal small tree for drought-friendly, minimal-care landscapes. Deciduous. Drought Tolerant. Easy Care. Year-round Interest. North American Native Selection. Waterwise. Attracts Hummingbirds. Fall Color. Attractive Bark. Bird Friendly.</p>
