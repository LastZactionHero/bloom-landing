      <p>A unique dwarf, hybrid variety of hardy hibiscus with dainty little white flowers punctuated with a showy red eye. Late summer flowers last for up to three days instead of one day that is typical of most Rose of Sharon, and are less likely to produce viable, unwanted seedlings. Lovely in containers or as a hedge or specimen. Dwarf Habit. Fast Growing. Attracts Butterflies. Deer Resistant. Dwarf Plant. Tolerates Road Salt. Bird Friendly.</p>
